Our Mission

Daddy's Chariot Inc provides essential support services to fathers without means who are working a Case Plan under an open DCF/Child Welfare case. We remove practical barriers — transportation, hygiene, resources — so fathers can focus on reunification with their children.

About Daddy's Chariot Inc

Daddy's Chariot Inc is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to helping fathers navigate the practical and emotional challenges of an open DCF/Child Welfare case. Many fathers working a Case Plan lack reliable transportation, stable housing, or the resources to present themselves well at hearings and visits — we exist to close that gap.

We proudly partner with Fatherhood Engagement Hub™ to support fathers across every aspect of their journey — from practical case needs (our focus) to emotional and relational support (their focus).

Our Programs

Transportation

Rides to and from doctor's appointments, court dates, job interviews/work, and other case-required destinations.

Visitation Bags

Bags stocked with essentials and goodies to support meaningful, prepared visits with children.

Traveling Shower, Closet & Grooming

Hygiene, clothing, and grooming support so fathers can present themselves with dignity at hearings and visits.

Housing Assistance

Connections to housing assistance and support resources for fathers working toward stability.

Food Assistance

Connections to food assistance and support resources for fathers and their families.

Employment Support

Job readiness support and connections to employment opportunities.

Education & Job Training

Support connecting fathers to education and job training opportunities.

Legal Navigation Support

Guidance and support navigating legal entanglements related to family and child welfare cases.
